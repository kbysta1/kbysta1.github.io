Privacy Policy

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how keepgamesimple LLC (“we”, “our” or “us”) collects, uses, stores, shares and otherwise processes personal information when you use our mobile applications, games, websites and any related services (collectively, the “Services”).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Device information such as OS-generated IDs, device model and other device identifiers.
  • Usage data on our website (time on site, clickstream, purchase history) and within our apps (session start time, in-app events, purchase history).
  • Cookie IDs or similar technologies generated through your browser or device.

3. Purposes of Processing

We use the information for:

  1. Managing and authenticating your use of the Services.
  2. Analyzing user behaviour to maintain and improve the Services.
  3. Preventing and responding to violations of our Terms or the law.
  4. Operating, protecting and enhancing the Services overall.
  5. Delivering, displaying and measuring advertising.
  6. Showing personalised/interest-based ads.
  7. Processing payments or settlement of fees.

4. Legal Bases for EEA/UK Residents

If you are located in the EEA or UK, we process your personal data on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide the Services you request.
  • Legitimate interests – for analytics, fraud prevention and network security.
  • Consent – for optional activities such as personalised advertising where required.
  • Compliance with legal obligations – when we must keep certain records.

You may withdraw consent at any time (see “Your Rights” below).

5. Security Measures

We implement administrative, technical and physical safeguards appropriate to the risk of processing. Details of specific measures can be provided upon request through the contact information at the end of this Policy.

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share personal data with third parties (including those outside Japan) without your prior consent, except:

  • To trusted service providers acting under contract on our behalf.

Analytics & Advertising Partners
